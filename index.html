We present to you our Art Portfolio

People appreciate the usefulness of things like glassware and furniture, but they appreciate such objects even more when they’re aesthetically pleasing, too. That’s where we comes in. Our group explores the world of basketry, metalwork, pottery, interior design, tapestry, and more.

We are self taught kenyan artists with over 3 years experience in the creative world.We've had an amazing journey coming to where we are today and now feel confident that we can provide professionalism in the world of art.

We consider art as the process or product of deliberately arranging elements in a way that appeals to the senses or emotions. We encompasse a diverse range of human activities, creations and ways of expression, including music, literature, film, sculpture and paintings.

Creative Styles

We create...

Decorative Art

We create unique artistic pieces you can use to decorate your personal or professional space.Our decorative pieces focus on vintage and temporary themes.The decorative arts are arts or crafts whose object is the design and manufacture of objects that are both beautiful and functional. It includes most of the arts making objects for the interiors of buildings, and interior design, but not usually architecture. Ceramic art, metalwork, furniture, jewellery, fashion, various forms of the textile arts and glassware are major groupings.

Fine Art

Using paintings,We create dine art that focuses on presenting both the beauty of the world as we see it,and beauty as conceived by the mind.Fine art is art developed primarily for aesthetics or beauty, distinguishing it from decorative art or applied art, which also has to serve some practical function, such as pottery or most metalwork. The highest art is that which allows the full expression and display of the artist's imagination, unrestricted by any of the practical considerations involved in, say, making and decorating a teapot. It is also considered important that making the artwork does not involve dividing the work between different individuals with specialized skills, as might be necessary with a piece of furniture, for example.Even within the fine arts, there is a hierarchy of genres based on the amount of creative imagination required.

Visual Art

Here we capitalize on the power of the eye and mind.We draw paintings that brings you closer to nature,events, imaginations, and emotions and the world.The visual arts are art forms such as painting, drawing, printmaking, sculpture, ceramics, photography, video, filmmaking, design, crafts, and architecture. Many artistic disciplines such as performing arts, conceptual art, and textile arts also involve aspects of visual arts as well as arts of other types. Also included within the visual arts are the applied arts such as industrial design, graphic design, fashion design, interior design and decorative art.
